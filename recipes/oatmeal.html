Oatmeal

Description

A simple breakfast for when you're in a rush.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Milk or substitute
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Put oatmeal in a bowl and cover it with water.
  2. Let it rest overnight.
  3. In the morning, put the oatmeal in a pot and add milk.
  4. Warm it up.
  5. Put it back in the bowl and add maple syrup.
