Pasta with Ragu

Description

Pasta with ragu is an Italian classic. 'Nough said. Try it.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Grandma
  • Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Ask your grandma to make that delicious ragu sauce that only they know how to make.
  2. Put some water on the stove, salt it, and set the stove on High.
  3. Put the ragu in a pot and warm it up.
  4. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and let boil as needed.
  5. Drain the pasta, add the ragu, thank your grandma, and enjoy!
