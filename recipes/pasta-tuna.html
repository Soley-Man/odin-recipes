Pasta with Tuna

Description

This delicious pasta dish with tuna and tomato sauce is your favourite Italian's favourite. Don't let the seafood part scare you: it's as easy to enjoy as it is to make.

Tip: If someone shows reluctance when you mention this dish, just make it and let them smell it. They will fall in love.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Canned tuna
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Basil (opional)
  • NO Parmesan, you weirdo. Seafood and cheese don't mix.

Steps

  1. Put some water on the stove, salt it, and set the stove on High.
  2. Pour tomato sauce in a pan and salt it.
  3. Cut a slice of garlic into thin pieces and add it to the sauce.
  4. Let the sauce warm on medium heat.
  5. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta.
  6. Let boil as needed. Turn off the heat under the sauce once it starts boiling.
  7. Add a can of tuna and some basil in the sauce.
  8. Drain the pasta, add the sauce, and enjoy!
